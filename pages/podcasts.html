It's Corona Time

with Kevin Jackson

Due to the current social climate, we have decided to dive head first into a new medium, the world of podcasting. It's Corona Time is a 91BTV podcast where our host, Kevin Jackson talks with various peers, friends, and local YouTubers regarding their personal journeys in life and take a look at the current cultural, and societal climate in a comedic way. It comes in a video as well as an audio form.
